Get answers to common questions

How long does it take to see results?

Once you mail your dispute letters, credit bureaus have 30 days to investigate (up to 45 days in some cases). Most clients see their first deletions within 45 days of mailing their letters.

Is credit repair legal?

Yes, 100% legal. The Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) gives you the legal right to dispute any inaccurate, unverifiable, or unfair information on your credit report. We help you exercise that right effectively.
